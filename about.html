Our Journey

Building Connections Since 2010

Founded with a vision to revolutionize freight brokerage, Velocity Logistics has grown from a small team of logistics enthusiasts to a leading industry player. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make freight management simpler, more efficient, and more transparent for everyone involved.

Over the years, we've cultivated relationships with thousands of carriers and shippers, developing a deep understanding of the unique challenges in the transportation industry. Our technology-driven approach combined with personalized service allows us to deliver exceptional results for clients across diverse industries.

Today, we move over 20,000 loads monthly across North America, but we still maintain the personal touch that built our reputation. We're not just moving freight; we're building partnerships that drive mutual success.
